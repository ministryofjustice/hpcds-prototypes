Reviewing patterns

The process we will use for reviewing, feeding back and including new patterns.

How the design panel reviews patterns

This are the criteria the design panel will look at when reviewing each pattern. We do this to ensure we are making the designs as useful and transferable across services and not repeating any lessons learnt.

  1. 1  Has the design solved the problem?
  2. 2  Is there more than one example of a problem being solved?
  3. 3  Has it been simplified down?
  4. 4  Is there something which solves the user need already in the library?
  5. 5  Is it appropriate?
  6. 6  Has the pattern been researched and tested?
  7. 7  Has the pattern been reviewed from an access needs perspective?
