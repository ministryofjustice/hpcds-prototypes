2. Submissions

All submissions must be made in a timely manner and as such no claims should be submitted after 6 months from the date the client received help at court.

If the HPCDS fee has already been claimed before the legal help matter is opened then you should notify hpcds@legalaid.gsi.gov.uk of the need to amend the report. The Provider Assurance team will retrospectively tick the ‘Legal Help’ field in the relevant report, make a note in the ‘notes’ field and recoup the fee paid. You should report the Legal Help claim in the usual way.

Please note that HPCDS cases which go on to receive Legal Help will still be counted as HPCDS acts of assistance for the purposes of your allocation and performance.

Payments are triggered by our receipt of fully completed monthly reporting spreadsheet (HPCDS Contract Report Form v1.4) on or before their respective due date. Payment will be delayed if reports are not received on time. Guidance is available at Annex 4 which offers help with how to complete the monthly reporting spreadsheet.

Submit online

You can submit your claim online. The online service will:

  • reminder and confirmation notifications
  • check the data for errors
  • tell you how much and when to expect your payment
  • display historical claims

Submit via email

You can submit via email. This is a more basic service and you should expect longer payment periods.

Send your contract report form to hpcdsreports@legalaid.gsi.gov.uk
